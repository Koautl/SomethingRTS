What is SomethingRTS?

SomethingRTS is a is a simple real time stragey game. In which you and your opponent try to destroy eachother's headquarters. by producing attacking units through the baracks structure found in the game. The start of the game has both players produce a miner unit that mines gold nodes on the map. As the miner mines gold, the player gets to decide what he does with said gold, either building more miners to speed up his economy or creating a barracks structure so they can produce attacking units before their opponent so they put early pressure on them

HOW DO I PLAY?

You start off with your HQ at 100 HP and 60 gold. Once you click on the HQ you can create a miner unit or create a barracks. Miners cost 20 gold and are represented by an orange pixel. Melee units cost 10 gold and are represented by a blue pixel, they match the speed of the enemy units and deal the same amount of damge which is 4. Tanks cost 25 gold, move half the speed as a melee but deal 10 damage to the enmy hq. Tanks are represented by a purple pixel . Rangers cost 15 and deal 6 damage to enemy hq but don't have to get as close the tank or Melee, before doing damage. Rangers are represented by a blue pixel. The enemy units will deal more damage as the game goes on to keep games very short.
